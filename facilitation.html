What is an IC Agreement?

  • It's an agreement between you and a company or firm
  • It states the services you'll provide, the deadlines, and the payment method.

Why do I need an IC Agreement

  • So you have proof of the job you did
  • To protect yourself from the IRS

Advantages to having an IC Agreement

  • You look professional
  • It assures you get paid
  • You have a clear defined guideline of what you are going to do
